DIY Solar Generator: Parts & Tools

Published: 2025-02-05

Since parts/tools and prices change over time, this is mainly a reference of what I used. You should do your own research to ensure you're getting what you need to fit your needs or budget.

I've roughly ordered items by usage, so you can tackle things incrementally and not purchase everything at once.

Tools
ToolUsage
Ansell ActivArmr Multipurpose GlovesWorn when handling anything on a live circuit.
Jonard Tools CST-1900 Round Cable StripperFor stripping cable jackets off thick gauge wire.
TEMCo TH0006 Hydraulic Electrical Cable Lug/Terminal Crimper KitUsed to crimp larger connecters on cables.
Cable Lug Crimper with Wire CutterUsed this until I couldn't crimp some Anderson connector pins, then I got the hydraulic crimper. The wire cutter worked well, but if you already have one you can skip this.
10-24 AWG Ratchet Wire CrimperCrimps connectors to smaller wires.
MYTEC Clamp-On ViseUsed for bending lugs and holding on to the cutting board while I cut it to bits.
Klein Tools 11063W Automatic Wire Cutter/Stripper, for 8-20 AWG Solid and 10-22 AWG Stranded Electrical WireFor stripping cable jackets off smaller gauge wire.
DEWALT 20V Multitool KitUsed for cutting plastic and metal.
Multitool Blade KitBlades for cutting different surfaces. Specifically the blade for plastics, and the round blade are what I used.
Lenox 13 Piece Bi-Metal Hole Saw KitTo cut holes in case for front-facing electronics.
Parts
PartUsage
RIDGID Rolling Tool BoxThe case for everything.
Redodo 12V 100Ah Mini LiFePO4 Battery (x3)Hook 3 together in array for 300Ah.
Redodo 14.6V 40A LiFePO4 AC Battery ChargerUsed for charging the batteries up via AC.
2-10 AWG Silicone Terminal CoversUsed over the top of the Invertor terminals. Also use them when doing maintenance on the battery array to ensure if a cable flopped over on another terminal it wouldn't cause a short.
4 AWG - 5/16" (M8) Tinned Copper LugsLugs for wires to connect to battery.
Sanuke 2-8 AWG Lugs KitUsed for battery cables. Ended up running out of the 4AWG 5/16" and bought the individual ones (which ended up being better), and 2 4AWG 3/8" were used for the Shunt terminals - otherwise you can skip this kit and buy what you need.
Steel Metal RodsUsed for bending lugs to the angles I needed.
iGreely 4 AWG Wire 10ft Tinned CopperFor wiring up battery array.
M8 Hex Nuts & BoltsUsed to mount 300A fuse to battery.
Blue Sea Systems 300 Amp MEGA/AMG FuseFuse between battery and circuit breaker.
Nut, bolt, screw gaugeDetermine what size parts you need in case the sizes aren't apparent. Didn't have this during the build, but wish I did.
Screw, Bolt, Nut, Washers KitUsed for mounting the bus bars, circuit breaker, and shunt.
300A Bus Bar 12v Power Distribution BlockSince space was a concern, these allowed for wiring components to the sides of the case instead of on top of a battery. I can also take the batteries in an out without having to unhook everything.
300 Amp 2-in-1 Manual Reset Circuit BreakerUsed as a battery shut-off, as well as the usual circuit breaker protection.
12 AWG Wire BNTECHGO 10ft Flexible Tinned CopperFor wiring components.
16 AWG wireUsed for wiring lower amperage parts.
Renogy Battery Monitor with Shunt, 10V-120V up to 500ATo view status of battery from front of case.
WAGO Lever Nuts for 12-24 AWGTo easily connect/splice wires together. You can choose to keep them in or solder your connections.
Heat Shrink Wire Connectors KitFor small wire connections. I ended up getting some specific connectors since this kit didn't have many of what I ended up needing.
HKS 10-12 AWG Female Spade Quick Disconnect ConnectorsUsed to connect higher amperage parts.
Sanuke 10-12 AWG 5/16" Ring Terminals Heat Shrink Wire ConnectorsUsed to connect higher amperage parts to bus bar.
MUYI 12 AWG 30 Amp Inline Fuse HolderFuses between external panels to bus bar.
BioFuse 3-30 Amp ATO FusesI prefer fuses with clear markings.
2-Way Switch PanelFor mounting toggle switches. Be mindful of cutouts and notches to ensure your switches fit.
Nilight Round 12V LED Toggle SwitchesTo turn temperature control units on/off.
12V Programmable Temperature ControllersTurns fans or heater on depending on specified temperatures.
Kohree 12"x18" 12V/13.5V RV Water Tank Heating PadFor heating up the batteries in case the temperature drops too low. Some batteries have built in heating functions but cost more and add to the size.
12V Moisture-Proof 120mm FansFor cooling the inside of the case.
120mm Aluminum Fan GrillsDust filter and protects the fans. Wish I'd bought non-aluminum since I needed to stick magnetic covers on them.
iGreely 10 AWG Wire 10ft Tinned CopperFor wiring components.
Haerkn 10 AWG Wire 10ft Tinned CopperFor wiring components. Bought this on accident. It was stiffer than the other.
14 AWG 3 Core Extension Cable 10ftFor making custom extension cables.
NEMA 5-15P Electrical Extension Cord Ends 125V 15A 2Pole 3Wire Male PlugUsed for custom extension cord from Invertor to AC receptacles.
STARELO 15 Amp Power Receptacle NEMA 5-15R with Cover, 125Volt 2Pole 3WireAC receptacles to plug equipment or power strips into.
50A Anderson SB50 Plug Mounting PanelsUsed the mounting panels but with the other Anderson connectors
10-12 AWG Anderson ConnectorsUsed for connecting solar panels and AC charger. Had to buy these for the pins since I messed up crimping the ones that came with the receptacles.
MUYI 10 AWG 40 Amp Inline Fuse HolderFuse between solar panels and Solar Charge Controller.
BioFuse 40 Amp ATO FusesI prefer fuses with clear markings.
30 Ohm 25 Watt ResistorUse before hooking Invertor up to battery to prevent spark (I had mixed results with this).
Plastic Cutting Board, 12x18 InchFor mounting the Solar Charge Controller and Inverter. Should've gone with a piece peg-board since I ended up cutting a majority of it away.
Teflon SheetsUsed as a heat/fire/electrical barrier between certain components.
2500W 12v Pure Sine Wave InverterConverts DC from the battery to AC and sends it to the front-panel AC receptacles.
Victron MPPT Solar Charge Controller (Bluetooth) - 100V, 30 amp, 12/24-VoltTo charge battery from solar panel(s).
Nilight 4 in 1 Panel, USB, Voltmeter, Cigarette Lighter SocketDC front-panel mainly used for USB charging but the cigarette lighter socket could be useful.
tesa 4970 Double-Sided PVC Film Tape, 0.5in x 60ydFor mounting cable ties and foam padding.
Cable Tie MountsAllows for easier organizing of cables within case.
Acorn Nuts M3-M10Caps for screws or bolts that stick out of case.
10 AWG 50A/600V Solar to Anderson Connector, 2ft CableTo connect solar panels to front-panel receptacle.
Magnetic vent coversCut to fit as cover plates for fans. If you kept the plastic cut out from the case for the fans you may be able to use those instead.
N52 Neodymium MagnetsUsed to pin cover plate over fans. If you don't care about covers or yours are staying on fine, then skip these.
20A 12 AWG 8-Outlet Metal Power Strip Surge Protector 6ftHeavy duty power strip that you can plug multiple items into. Keep in mind that the outlets are standard household 15A and can only deliver so much power before tripping a circuit.
Yellow Jacket 50ft 10AWG 20A Extension CordThis'll save you from lugging the generator around. This combined with the power strip should give you power a good distance away.
4-in-1 15 Amp AC Plug to 20 Amp T-Blade AdapterTo use the long heavy-duty extension cord in a normal AC outlet.
RIDGID Modular Tool BoxOptional storage that stacks and locks onto the main case. I have other emergency supplies in it, and can wheel the whole thing around.
